Design Definitions

Contrast

Contrast principle is used to design webpages so that each element has an appealing appearance and stands out from the background. For these webpages it is achievedÂ by adding a white overlay layer on top of the image.

Repetition

The repetition principle is achieved by ensuring that each element on the webpage has a display box based on its category. The root.html used similar display boxes for the table list and hyperlinks.

Alignment

Every element of all the webpages have a center alignment for a clean visual connection to follow through.

Proximity

A clear structure with closeness and proximity has been established to ensure that the elements are recognized as members of a specific group. The table parts are in horizontal proximity, and the hyperlinks are in vertical proximity.
